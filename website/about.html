About Cindy

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind the voice research assistant.

Our Story

Cindy was born out of a simple need: to make research more accessible and efficient. Our founder, a researcher struggling with information overload, envisioned a voice assistant that could help navigate the vast sea of knowledge with natural conversations.

What started as a personal project quickly grew into a mission to help researchers, students, and knowledge workers everywhere focus on what matters most - the insights, not the process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to knowledge by creating intuitive voice interfaces that make research seamless and enjoyable. We believe that technology should enhance human intelligence, not replace it.

We're committed to building tools that respect user privacy, prioritize accuracy, and continuously learn to better serve our users' research needs.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: Your research belongs to you. We never store or share your data without explicit permission.
  • Accuracy Matters: We prioritize factual correctness and proper citation in all research outputs.
  • Accessibility: Knowledge should be available to everyone, regardless of ability or circumstance.
  • Continuous Learning: We're always improving, learning from user feedback and technological advances.
