Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan with full access to all features.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, save 20% when you choose annual billing for the Professional plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I use Cindy offline?

Yes, all plans include offline mode with local models. The Professional and Enterprise plans include enhanced models for better offline performance.

How does the knowledge vault work?

The knowledge vault automatically saves and indexes your research. You can search your personal vault to retrieve information from past research sessions.
